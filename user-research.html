User Research

01 Findings
- Residents are mostly the elderly and low-income groups.
- Many residents are willing to cooperate with the government to renovate their houses and living conditions. Living conditions often refer to the bathroom and other functional rooms.
- Residents are generally indifferent to the protection of the surrounding environment.
02 Quotes from The Wongs who are Pancake Store owners and common residents.
- "I'm willing to renew my house if the government would subsidize it. As for the other space, I don't have extra money for it"
- "The pancakes are mainly sold to the residents. Most commercials here are scattered"
03 Quotes from Zhenrong Cao who is an inheritor of the intangible cultural heritage of Qinghuai Lantern
‍- "The current situation of the production base is worrying. I've been 75 years old and I hope someone could pass on this craft, and let more people know about and love it."
