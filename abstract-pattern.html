4th round of data analysis

- describe the existing user experience through User Journey Map
- combine user research and User Journey Map to summarize the insights


Insights
◼ Full-time working parents value high-quality parent-child accompany hours during family journeys.
◼ They tend to sacrifice their own needs to keep children entertained and educated during journeys.
◼ Parents feel stressed when keeping attention on the child at all times for safety, health, and playing issues due to children's complex needs, short attention span, and their own unnecessary worries.
