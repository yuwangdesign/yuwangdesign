SUS

The System Usability Scale (SUS) is a 10-item questionnaire scored on a 100-point scale and provides a measure of a user's perception of the usability of a system (Sauro, 2018). Acceptable corresponds to a score above 70, unacceptable to below 50, and marginally acceptable to 50-70 (Bangor et al, 2008). A total of 78 people complete the SUS survey.

NPS

The Net Promoter Score (NPS) designates 3 classes of recommenders based on their responses to the 11-points (0 to 10) likelihood to recommend questions. Promoters score 9 and 10; Passives, 8 and 7 nd Detractors, 6 and below (Sauro, 2018). 80 participants involved.
